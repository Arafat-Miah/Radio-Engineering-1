# Radio Engineering I – Homework Solutions

This repository contains the **homework questions and my corresponding solutions** for the course **Radio Engineering I** at the **University of Oulu**.

The repository is maintained as a record of my coursework and learning in **RF and microwave engineering**.

---

## Course Overview

Radio Engineering I covers the fundamentals of **radio-frequency and microwave circuit analysis and design**. The course focuses on both theoretical understanding and practical problem-solving related to RF front-end systems.

---

## Repository Contents

- Homework question PDFs provided during the course  
- My written solution PDFs for each homework  

Each homework includes the **original problem statement** and my **detailed solution**.

---

## Topics Covered

- Transmission lines and distributed circuit models  
- Reflection coefficient and impedance transformation  
- Impedance matching using lumped elements and microstrip lines  
- Smith Chart–based matching techniques  
- Resonators and quality factor  
- Power dividers and directional couplers  
- Low Noise Amplifier (LNA) design  
- S-parameters, gain, and stability analysis  
- Noise sources, noise figure, and cascade analysis  
- Nonlinearity and dynamic range  
- Power amplifiers and mixers
